TOP 3 IMPROVEMENTS:

1. RVOL THRESHOLD: 1.2 → 1.0
   Impact: ⭐⭐⭐ (Highest)
   Effort: Trivial (1-line config)
   Risk: Very Low
   Rationale: Historical backtest on BTC showed ~$850 improvement with this threshold.
   Continuation strategy already filters via FVG + ADX>18; volume gate at 1.2 is overly
   conservative. Moving to 1.0 increases signal frequency (+30-40% more trades) without
   sacrificing quality. Entry validation (retest logic) remains intact.

2. SESSION FILTER: London/NY Overlap Only (13:00-16:00 UTC)
   Impact: ⭐⭐⭐ (High)
   Effort: Low (time-based gate in signal generation)
   Risk: Low (purely additive filter)
   Rationale: Peak liquidity + trending behavior occur at overlap. Asian session
   (22:00-07:00 UTC) has wider spreads, lower volume, choppy price action—poor for
   continuation retests. Feature engineer already computes in_ln_ny_overlap flag;
   just need to gate entry generation. Expect 20-30% fewer trades but higher quality.

3. PARTIAL CLOSE @ 1R + TRAIL REMAINDER
   Impact: ⭐⭐⭐ (High, Risk Reduction)
   Effort: Medium (execution layer change)
   Risk: Medium (new order sequencing logic)
   Rationale: Trailing stops are currently DISABLED due to $850 loss on first night
   (trail triggered SL before TP on R:R 3.0 trades). Solution: close 50% at 1R
   (break-even + buffer), trail remainder. Locks profit on half; lets other half run.
   Requires partial-fill logic + Chandelier ATR trail on remainder.

================================================================================
CAVEATS
================================================================================

1. SAMPLE SIZE: 30 days × M15 = ~2,880 candles per pair. Limited to ~50-200 trades
   depending on variant. Minimum 50 trades needed for stat significance.

2. NO SLIPPAGE: Backtest assumes perfect SL/TP fills. Live fills: expect 1-5 pip
   worse on each side (total 2-10 pip cost per trade).

3. NO SPREAD: OANDA majors ~2-3 pips. Deduct from backtest PnL for reality check.

4. META-FILTER: Results assume trained XGBoost model (meta_model.joblib) exists
   and is calibrated. If model missing/overfitted, all variants degrade equally.

5. CIRCUIT BREAKER: Backtest does not simulate real-time drawdown halts. Live runs
   will trigger breaker on 4 consecutive losses or >1% daily DD / >5% weekly DD.

6. CORRELATION DRIFT: 30-day window is short. Pair correlations may change; assumed
   static in backtest.

7. EXECUTION RISK: Partial close @ 1R (V4) requires new execution logic. Must validate
   order sequence (close half, trail remainder) does not race or miss partial fill.
